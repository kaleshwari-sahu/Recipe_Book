Chousela

Details

Serves: 4-6

To Prep: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

To Cook: 30 minutes

Nutrition

Calories: 180-220 kcal

Protein: 4-5g

Carbohydrates: 30-35g

Fat: 5-7g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup jaggery (gud), grated
  • 1/2 cup water (to melt jaggery)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. In a pan, add jaggery and water. Heat it until the jaggery melts completely. Strain to remove any impurities and let it cool slightly.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, cardamom powder, and a pinch of salt. Add the jaggery syrup and mix well to form a dough. You can also add grated coconut to the dough if desired.
  3. Knead the dough until smooth and divide it into small portions.
  4. Shape each portion into a small ball and flatten it slightly.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry the flattened dough balls until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  7. Remove them from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve Chousela warm or at room temperature as a sweet snack.
