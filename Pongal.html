pongal

Details

Serves: 4

To Prep: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

To Cook: 30 minutes

Nutrition per serving

Calories: 250-300 kcal

Protein: 6-8g

Carbohydrates: 45-50g

Fat: 5-7g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (split yellow gram)
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tbsp cashew nuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Method

  1. Dry roast the moong dal in a pan until it turns light golden and aromatic. Set aside.
  2. Wash the rice and roasted moong dal together, then drain the water.
  3. In a pressure cooker, combine the rice, moong dal, 4 cups of water, and salt. Cook for 3-4 whistles until the rice and dal are soft.
  4. In a separate pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds, black peppercorns, asafoetida, grated ginger, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  5. Add cashew nuts and fry until they turn golden brown.
  6. Pour the tempering over the cooked rice and dal mixture, mix well, and simmer for a few minutes on low heat.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
