paneer_butter_masala

Details

Serves: 4-6

To Prep: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

To Cook: 30 minutes

Nutrition

Calories: 400-450 kcal

Protein: 10-12g

Carbohydrates: 20-25g

Fat: 30-35g

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked and ground to a paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Method

  1. Heat butter in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them crackle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Mix in the cashew nut paste and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the cubed paneer and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Add fresh cream, garam masala, and sugar. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
