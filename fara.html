Fara

Details

Serves: 6-8

To prep: 10

Difficulty: Easy

To cook: 20

Nutrition per serving

Calories: 250-300

Protein: 8-10g

Carbohydrates: 30-35g

Fat: 12-15g

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Green chilies, chopped
  • Cumin seeds

Method

  1. Soak chana dal for a few hours, then grind to a coarse paste.
  2. Mix rice flour with the dal paste, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, green chilies, curry leaves, and salt.
  3. Form the mixture into small balls or shapes.
  4. Steam the balls for 15-20 minutes.
