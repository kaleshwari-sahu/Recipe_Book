Barfi Malai

Details

Serves: 6-8

To Prep: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

To Cook: 30 minutes

Nutrition

Calories: 250-300kcal

Protein: 6-8g

Carbohydrates: 35-40g

Fat: 12-15g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full-fat milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup grated khoya (mawa)
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped mixed nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • Silver leaf (chandi ka varak) for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. In a large pan, heat ghee over medium heat.
  2. Add grated khoya and saut√© for a few minutes until it turns light golden.
  3. Add milk and sugar to the pan. Stir continuously until the mixture thickens.
  4. Sprinkle cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using) into the mixture. Continue to cook until it reaches a barfi-like consistency.
  5. Grease a plate or tray with ghee and pour the mixture into it. Spread evenly and press chopped nuts on top.
  6. Allow the barfi to cool and set. Once set, cut it into square pieces.
  7. If using, garnish with silver leaf (chandi ka varak) for an elegant touch.
  8. Serve the Barfi Malai at room temperature as a delightful sweet treat.
