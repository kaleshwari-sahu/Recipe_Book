Paya Soup

Paya Soup

Details

Serves: 4-6

To Prep: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

To Cook: 2-3 hours

Nutrition

Calories: 150-200kcal

Protein: 15-20g

Carbohydrates: 5-10g

Fat: 8-10g

Ingredients

  • 4-6 goat trotters (paya), cleaned
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water as needed
  • 2-3 lemon wedges (for serving)

Method

  1. Heat ghee in a large pot and sauté the sliced onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add turmeric powder, black pepper powder, and garam masala powder. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring continuously.
  4. Add the cleaned goat trotters (paya) and sauté for 5-7 minutes until well coated with the spices.
  5. Add enough water to cover the trotters and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover the pot, and simmer for 2-3 hours until the trotters are tender and the soup is flavorful.
  6. Add slit green chilies and salt to taste. Simmer for another 15-20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with lemon wedges on the side.
