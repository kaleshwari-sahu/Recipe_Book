Serves: 4

Difficulty: Easy

To cook: 15 min

Nutrition per serving

Calories: 150

Protein: 3g

Carbohydrates: 28g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • tsp salt'
  • Water as needed
  • 'Oil for cooking

Method

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, grated coconut, cumin seeds, and salt.',
  2. Gradually add water and knead to form a soft dough..
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them into thick discs.',
  4. 'Heat a tawa (griddle) and cook the rotis on medium heat, applying oil on both sides until they are golden brown.'
