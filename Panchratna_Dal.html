Panchratna Dal

Mixed Lentil Delight

Details

Serves: 4-6

To Prep: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

To Cook: 45 minutes

Nutrition

Calories: 300-350 kcal (per serving)

Protein: 15-20g

Carbohydrates: 40-45g

Fat: 10-12g

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup split chickpeas (chana dal)
  • 1/4 cup split yellow lentils (moong dal)
  • 1/4 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 1/4 cup black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma), soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Method

  1. Drain the soaked kidney beans. Cook all the lentils and kidney beans in a pressure cooker with water until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee or oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Mix in the cooked lentils and kidney beans. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, adding water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  6. Stir in fresh cream and garam masala. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
