Vada

Details

Serves: 4-6

To Prep: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

To Cook: 20 minutes

Calories: 150-200 kcal

Protein: 6-8g

Carbohydrates: 15-20g

Fat: 8-10g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram), soaked for 4-5 hours
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns, crushed
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 sprig curry leaves, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Water as needed

Method

  1. Drain the soaked urad dal and grind it to a smooth paste using minimal water.
  2. Transfer the batter to a large bowl and beat it well to make it light and fluffy.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chilies, cumin seeds, black pepper, ginger, curry leaves, and salt to the batter. Mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Wet your hands with water, take a small portion of the batter, and shape it into a round vada with a hole in the center.
  6. Carefully slide the vada into the hot oil. Fry a few vadas at a time, turning them occasionally until they are golden brown and crisp.
  7. Remove the vadas using a slotted spoon and drain the excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
